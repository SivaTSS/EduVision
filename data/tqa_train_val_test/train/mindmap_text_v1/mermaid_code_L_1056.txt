mindmap
  Ultrasound
    Definition
      - Sound frequency > 20,000 Hz
      - Not detectable by human ear
    Animal Echolocation
      - Used by bats and dolphins
      - Identifies objects in the dark
      - Finds prey and avoids obstacles
    Sonar
      - Stands for Sound Navigation and Ranging
      - Locates underwater objects (e.g., submarines)
      - Functions as sender and receiver
      - Distance calculation using speed and time
        - Example: Distance = Speed x Time
    Ultrasonography
      - Uses reflected ultrasound waves
      - Creates images of internal organs
      - Safe for patients (e.g., unborn babies)
