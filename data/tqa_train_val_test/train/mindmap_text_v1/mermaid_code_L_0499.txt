mindmap
  Diabetes
    Definition
      Non-infectious disease
      Inability to control blood sugar
    Types
      Type 1 Diabetes
        Causes
          Immune system attacks pancreas
        Characteristics
          Insulin production failure
          Develops in childhood/adolescence
          Requires insulin shots
        Symptoms
          Frequent blood sugar monitoring
          Extreme fatigue
          Weight loss
      Type 2 Diabetes
        Causes
          Insulin resistance in body cells
          Often linked to obesity
        Characteristics
          Pancreas may still produce insulin
          Develops in adulthood, increasingly in youth
        Symptoms
          Frequent urination
          Excessive thirst
          Blurry vision
    Risk Factors
      Genetic predisposition
      Obesity
      High blood pressure
    Common Symptoms
      Frequent urination
      Extreme thirst
      Extreme hunger
      Fatigue
      Blurry vision
      Slow healing of cuts
    Complications
      Eye complications
      Foot complications
      Skin complications
      High blood pressure
      Nerve damage
      Kidney disease
      Stroke
