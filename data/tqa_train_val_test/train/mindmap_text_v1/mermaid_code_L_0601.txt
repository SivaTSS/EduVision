mindmap
  Microscopes
    Purpose
      Tool for magnifying small objects
      Essential in biology
    Types
      Light Microscopes
        Uses lenses to focus light
        Magnification up to 2,000x
      Electron Microscopes
        Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEM)
          Uses electron beams
          Magnification up to 2,000,000x
        Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEM)
          Scans surface with electron beams
          Magnification from 10x to 500,000x
      Scanning Acoustic Microscopes
        Uses sound waves for scanning
    Historical Context
      Early Discoveries
        Zaccharias and Hans Janssen
          Experimented with lenses
        Robert Hooke
          Discovered cells in cork
        Antoine van Leeuwenhoek
          Discovered bacteria and protists
