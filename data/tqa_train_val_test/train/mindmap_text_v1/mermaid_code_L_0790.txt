mindmap
  acceleration
    Definition
      - Measure of change in velocity
      - Rate of change
      - Vector quantity
    Examples
      - Carousel rider
      - Skydiver in free fall
      - Driving around a bend
    Types of Acceleration
      - Positive acceleration (increase in speed)
      - Negative acceleration (deceleration)
      - Change in direction without speed change
    Effects of Acceleration
      - Sensation of change in speed
      - Feeling during car rides
        - Pressed against seat when speeding up
        - Pushed forward when slowing down
        - Pushed to the left during right turn
        - Pushed to the right during left turn
    Observations
      - Analyze velocity changes in examples
        - Direction changing but speed constant
        - Speed changing but direction constant
