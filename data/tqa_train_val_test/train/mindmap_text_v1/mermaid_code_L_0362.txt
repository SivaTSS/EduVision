mindmap
  Microscopes
    Definition
      Instrument for magnifying small objects
    Importance in Life Science
      Key discoveries enabled
        Cells and cell theory
        Bacteria and diseases
    History of Microscopes
      Invention
        Hans and Zacharias Jansen
          First light microscope (late 1500s)
      Key Contributors
        Robert Hooke
          First to observe living things
          Published "Micrographia"
          Discovered cells in cork
        Anton van Leeuwenhoek
          Improved microscope magnification
          First to observe bacteria and other microorganisms
    Types of Microscopes
      Light Microscopes
        Use lenses to refract light
        Magnification up to 2000x
        Common in labs
      Electron Microscopes
        Use beams of electrons
        Magnification up to 2 million times
        Required for viewing structures smaller than 550 nanometers

      Importance of following safety rules
