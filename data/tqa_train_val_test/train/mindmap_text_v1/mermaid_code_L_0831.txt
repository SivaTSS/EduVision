      Covalent Compounds
        Definition
          Nonmetals Joined by Covalent Bonds
        Example
          Hydrogen Peroxide
            Chemical Formula: H2O2
            Ratio: 2 H, 2 O
          Carbon Dioxide
            Chemical Formula: CO2
            Ratio: 1 C, 2 O
