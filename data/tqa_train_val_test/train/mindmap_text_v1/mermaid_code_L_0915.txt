mindmap
  Heat
    Definition
      - Transfer of thermal energy
      - Kinetic energy of moving particles
      - Measured by temperature
    Thermal Energy Transfer
      - Warmer to cooler substances
      - Equilibrium of temperature
      - Example: Oven heating food
    Practical Application
      - Hot cocoa in a cold mug
        - Thermal energy transfer explanation
      - Cooling cola with ice
        - Warm cola heats ice
        - Ice melts, cola cools
