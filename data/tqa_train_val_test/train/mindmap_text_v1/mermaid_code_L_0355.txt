mindmap
  Weathering and Erosion
    Weathering
      Definition
        Breaks down Earth materials
        Changes solid rock into sediments
      Process
        Disintegration of rock
        Formation of sediments
    Erosion
      Definition
        Transports sediments
      Relationship to Weathering
        Moves sediments after weathering
    Differences
      Weathering vs. Erosion
        Weathering: breakdown
        Erosion: transportation
    Time Factors
      Duration of Weathering
        Gradual process
        Long-term changes
      Examples
        Mountains to hills
        Appalachian Mountains history
    Observations
      Short-term changes
        Road wear over years
        Cracks, fractures, potholes
