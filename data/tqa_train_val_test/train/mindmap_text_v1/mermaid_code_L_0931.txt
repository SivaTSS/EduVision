mindmap
  Isomers
    Hydrocarbons
      Definition: Compounds of carbon and hydrogen
      Smallest Hydrocarbon: Methane (CH4)
      Larger Hydrocarbons: More carbon atoms = more isomers
    Types of Isomers
      Structural Isomers
        Butane
          Isomers: n-butane, iso-butane
          Formula: C4H10
        Pentane
          Isomers: n-pentane, iso-pentane, neo-pentane
          Formula: C5H12
      Other Hydrocarbons
        Heptane: 9 isomers
        Dodecane: 355 isomers
    Properties of Isomers
      Different arrangements = different properties
      Boiling and Melting Points
        Branched-chain vs. Straight-chain
        Example: 
          n-butane: 0°C, -138°C
          iso-butane: -12°C, -160°C
          Predictions for pentane isomers
            iso-pentane: 28°C
            neo-pentane: 10°C
