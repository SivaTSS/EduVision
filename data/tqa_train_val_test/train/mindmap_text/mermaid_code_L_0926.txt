    Combustion Engine
      Definition
        Complex machine
        Burns fuel for thermal energy

    Internal Combustion Engine
      Definition
        Burns fuel internally
        Found in most motor vehicles
      Working Principle
        Steps of operation
          Fuel-air mixture intake
          Compression of mixture
          Ignition by spark plug
          Expansion and downward piston movement
          Exhaust gas expulsion
          Cycle repetition
      Energy Conversion
        Thermal energy to kinetic energy

      Cylinder
      Piston
      Crankshaft
      Driveshaft
    Engine Configuration
      Number of Cylinders
        Four cylinders (common)
