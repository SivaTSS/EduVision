mindmap
  Unsaturated Hydrocarbons
    Definition
      Hydrocarbons with double or triple bonds
    Types
      Alkenes
        At least one double bond
        Naming convention
          Ends in -ene
          Prefix for number of carbon atoms
        Properties
          Different shapes
          Isomers
          Boiling and melting points
      Aromatic Hydrocarbons
        Cyclic hydrocarbons
        Structure
          Alternating single and double bonds
          Benzene as smallest example
        Uses
          Strong aroma
          Applications in air fresheners and mothballs
      Alkynes
        At least one triple bond
        Naming convention
          Ends in -yne
          Prefix for number of carbon atoms
        Properties
          Rare in nature
          Straight or branched chains
