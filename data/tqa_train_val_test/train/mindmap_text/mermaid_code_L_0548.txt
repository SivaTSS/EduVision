      Definition
        Breaking down food into nutrients
      Types

          Begins in mouth

      Accessory Organs
        Liver
        Pancreas
        Gallbladder

      Process
        Nutrients taken up by blood

      After absorption
        Nutrients travel to body cells
    Elimination
      Definition
        Passing solid waste out of the body
