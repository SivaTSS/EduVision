mindmap
  PNS
    Peripheral Nervous System
      Definition
        Nerves outside the CNS
        Includes nerves of limbs and organs
      Divisions
        Sensory Division
          Role
            Carries messages from sense organs to CNS
          Sensory Neurons
            Receive stimuli from environment
            Examples: sight, hearing, touch
        Motor Division
          Role
            Carries messages from CNS to muscles and organs
          Subdivisions
            Somatic Nervous System
              Controls voluntary movements
              Example: playing the violin
            Autonomic Nervous System
              Controls involuntary functions
              Subdivisions
                Sympathetic Division
                  "Fight or Flight" response
                  Prepares body for emergencies
                Parasympathetic Division
                  "Rest and Digest" response
                  Controls body during rest
