mindmap
  Continental Drift
    Alfred Wegener
      Background
        Meteorologist
        Explorer
        Born in 1880
      Key Events
        1910: Interest in continental movement
        1911: Discovery of identical fossils
        1915: Proposal of continental drift hypothesis
        1930: Continued research
        1960: Acceptance of theory
    Evidence for Hypothesis
      Fit of Continents
        Puzzle piece alignment
      Fossil Evidence
        Identical species on separate continents
        Examples: Glossopteris, Mesosaurus, Cynognathus, Lystrosaurus
      Geological Evidence
        Similar rock types and ages
        Mountain ranges: Appalachians and Greenland
      Climate Evidence
        Ancient glaciers in warm climates
        Coral reefs and coal seams in cold locations
    Criticism and Support
      Initial Skepticism
        Wegener labeled "crazy man"
        Need for more evidence
      Support from Colleagues
        Call for exploration and validation
