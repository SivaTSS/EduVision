mindmap
  Screw
    Definition
      Simple machine
      Inclined plane
      Central cylinder
    Uses
      Moving objects
        Greater depth
        Higher elevation
      Holding things together
        Wood pieces
        Bottle caps
    Examples
      Wood screw
      Bottle cap
      Other examples
    Mechanical Advantage
      Definition
        Output force > Input force
        Always greater than 1
      Factors
        Thread spacing
          Closer threads = easier to turn
          Widely spaced threads = harder to turn
        Trade-offs
          More turns needed
          Greater distance for input force
