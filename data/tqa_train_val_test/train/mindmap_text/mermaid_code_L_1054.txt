  Transverse Wave
    Definition
      Description: "Particles vibrate perpendicular to wave direction"
    Characteristics
      Crests: "High points of the wave"
      Troughs: "Low points of the wave"
    Examples
      Rope: "Wave travels down the rope as it is shaken"
      Guitar String: 
        Wave Direction: "Travels down the string"
        String Vibration: "Vibrates up and down"
    S Waves
      Definition: "Transverse waves occurring during earthquakes"
      Effects on Structures: "Can affect buildings as they travel through underground rocks"
