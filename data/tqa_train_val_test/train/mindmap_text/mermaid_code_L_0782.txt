mindmap
  Chemical Reactions
    Definition
      Process of changing substances
      Reactants and Products
        Reactants: starting substances
        Products: resulting substances
    Signs of Chemical Reactions
      Change in Color
      Change in Temperature
      Production of Gas
      Formation of Precipitate
    Chemical Reaction Process
      Bonds breaking in reactants
      New bonds forming in products
      Rearrangement of atoms
    Equilibrium
      Definition: balance between opposing changes
      Indicators of equilibrium
    Examples
      Vinegar and Baking Soda
      Hydrogen and Oxygen to Water
      Changes in Hair Care
    Non-Chemical Changes
      Changes of state (e.g., freezing, evaporation)
      No bonds broken, no new products formed
