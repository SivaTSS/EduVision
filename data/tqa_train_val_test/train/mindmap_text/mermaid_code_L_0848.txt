mindmap
  Cooling Systems
    Purpose
      Transfer thermal energy
      Keep things cool
    Examples
      Refrigerator
      Air Conditioner
    How a Refrigerator Works
      Thermal energy transfer
        From cool air to warm air
      Work
        Reverses normal heat flow
        Requires energy (electricity)
    Refrigerant
      Definition
        Low boiling point substance
        Changes between liquid and gas
      Function
        Absorbs thermal energy (liquid)
        Releases thermal energy (gas)
    Components of a Refrigerator
      Compressor
        Compresses refrigerant
      Condenser
        Cools refrigerant
      Evaporator
        Evaporates refrigerant
      Thermostat
        Controls temperature
