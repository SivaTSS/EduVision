mindmap
  Noble Gases
    Definition
      Nonreactive elements
      Group 18 of periodic table
    Properties
      Colorless and odorless
      Low boiling points
      Stable electron configuration
    Reactivity
      Almost nonreactive
      Octet rule
        Atoms form compounds for eight valence electrons
        Example: Sodium and chlorine reaction
    Elements
      Helium (He)
        Two electrons
        Exists as single atoms
      Neon (Ne)
      Argon (Ar)
      Krypton (Kr)
      Xenon (Xe)
      Radon (Rn)
        Radioactive
    Applications
      Balloons
        Helium-filled balloons float
      Light bulbs
        Argon prevents filament corrosion
      Lighted signs
        Different gases emit different colors
