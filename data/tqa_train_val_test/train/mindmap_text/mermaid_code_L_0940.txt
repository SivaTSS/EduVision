  Lipid Classification
    Lipids
      Definition
        Biochemical compounds
        Energy storage
      Types
        Fats
          Solid lipids
          Examples: butter, meat fats
        Oils
          Liquid lipids
          Examples: olive oil, vegetable oil
    Fatty Acids
      Structure
        Long carbon chains
      Types
        Saturated
          Single bonds
          Maximum hydrogen atoms
          Found in fats
        Unsaturated
          At least one double bond
          Fewer hydrogen atoms
          Found in oils
    Phospholipids
      Structure
        Contain phosphorus
        Two layers in cell membranes
      Function
        Hydrophilic and hydrophobic ends
        Selective permeability
