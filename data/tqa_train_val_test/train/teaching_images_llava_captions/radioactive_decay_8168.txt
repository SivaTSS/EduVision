Nitrogen-14 is an isotope of nitrogen with 14 protons and 14 neutrons in its nucleus. Nitrogen-14 is the most abundant isotope of nitrogen in the Earth's atmosphere, comprising about 74% of all nitrogen isotopes. Nitrogen-14 is a stable isotope, meaning that it does not undergo radioactive decay.
