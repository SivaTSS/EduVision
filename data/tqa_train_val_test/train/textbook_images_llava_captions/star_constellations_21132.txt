In the image, the night sky is illuminated by the glow of the Aurora Borealis, also known as the Northern Lights. The Aurora Borealis is a natural phenomenon that occurs when electrically charged particles from the sun interact with the Earth's magnetic field. The result is a mesmerizing display of colorful lights in the sky.
In this particular scene, the lights are arranged in the shape of a house, creating a unique and captivating visual effect. The house-shaped lights are scattered throughout the sky, with some closer to the ground and others higher up. The combination of the vibrant colors and the intricate design of the house-shaped lights make for an unforgettable viewing experience.
