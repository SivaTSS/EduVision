mindmap
  Excretion
    Definition
      Process of removing wastes from the body
    Organs Involved
      Kidneys
      Large Intestine
      Skin
      Lungs
    Waste Removal
      Gas
        Carbon Dioxide
      Liquid
        Urine
        Sweat
      Solid
        Feces
    Kidney Function
      Filters blood
      Forms urine
      Removes excess water, salts, nitrogen
    Homeostasis
      Maintains balance of fluids
      Affects urine color
    Other Organ Systems
      Respiratory System
        Lungs remove carbon dioxide
      Integumentary System
        Skin removes water and salts
      Digestive System
        Large intestine removes solid waste
      Urinary System
        Kidneys remove urea and excess water
