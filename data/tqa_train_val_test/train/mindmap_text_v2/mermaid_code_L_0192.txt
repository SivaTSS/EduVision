  Heat Waves and Droughts
    Heat Wave Definition
      Criteria
        More than five consecutive days
        Temperatures above 9 F (5 C) average
    Drought Definition
      Criteria
        Significantly less precipitation than normal
    Causes of Heat Waves
      High-pressure cell
      Jet stream position
        Jet stream further north than normal
    Consequences of Drought
      Soil moisture loss
      Dust storms
      Forest loss
      Wildlife disturbance
      Increased wildfires
    Recent Trends
      Increased frequency and duration
      Example: Summer 2011 North American heat wave
        Record temperatures
        States breaking temperature records
