mindmap
  Noble Gases
    Definition
      Nonreactive elements
      Group 18 of periodic table
      Colorless and odorless
      Low boiling points
    Reactivity
      Almost completely nonreactive
      Eight valence electrons
      Stable electron arrangement
      Exception: Helium (2 electrons)
    Octet Rule
      Atoms react to achieve eight valence electrons
      Standard for nonreactivity
      Example: Sodium and Chlorine reaction
    Uses
      Balloons
        Helium balloons float
      Light bulbs
        Argon prevents filament corrosion
      Lighted signs
        Different gases emit different colors
          Neon - reddish-orange
          Krypton - violet
          Xenon - blue
    Characteristics
      Noble gases include:
        Helium (He)
        Neon (Ne)
        Argon (Ar)
        Krypton (Kr)
        Xenon (Xe)
        Radon (Rn)
      Radon is radioactive
