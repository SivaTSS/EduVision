mindmap
  Heating Systems
    Basic Function
      Produce Thermal Energy
      Transfer Thermal Energy
    Types of Heating Systems
      Hot-Water Heating System
        Burns Fuel in Boiler
        Heats Water
        Pumps Hot Water
        Uses Pipes and Radiators
        Convection Currents
      Warm-Air Heating System
        Burns Fuel in Furnace
        Heats Air
        Forces Warm Air
        Uses Ducts and Registers
    Thermostat
      Monitors Temperature
      Triggers Boiler or Furnace
      Maintains Set Temperature
