mindmap
  Short Term Climate Change
    El Nino
      Characteristics
        Trade winds reverse
        Warm water pools off South America
        Upwelling stops
      Effects
        Collapse of food chain
        Increased rainfall in some regions
        Drought in other regions
    La Nina
      Characteristics
        Trade winds strengthen
        Colder water along South America
        Upwelling continues
      Effects
        More extreme than normal conditions
    ENSO
      Definition
        El Nino Southern Oscillation
      Cycle
        Occurs every 2 to 7 years
        Drives global climate changes
    Normal Conditions
      Trade winds blow east to west
      Low pressure in western Pacific
      Upwelling of nutrient-rich waters
