mindmap
  Fossilization Types
    Mold Fossils
      Definition: Depression left in the shape of the material
    Cast Fossils
      Definition: Rocky material that fills the mold
    Trace Fossils
      Definition: Evidence of organism activity
    True Form Fossils
      Definition: Original material preserved
    Preservation Methods
      Unaltered Preservation
        Example: Insects in amber
        Example: Mammoths in ice
      Permineralization
        Definition: Minerals replace organic material
        Examples: Dinosaur bones, petrified wood
    Fossil Formation Processes
      Compression
        Definition: High pressure leaves dark imprint
        Common in: Leaves and ferns
      Mineral Replacement
        Definition: Original material replaced by minerals
        Example: Calcite shells replaced by quartz
    Reasons for Few Soft Part Fossils
      Explanation: Soft parts decay quickly
      Exceptions: Insects in amber, animals in ice
