  Magnetic Field Reversal
    Definition
      Magnetic field reversal
      Earth's magnetic poles switch places
    Evidence
      Rocks on ocean floor
        Magnetic domains
        Alignment frozen in rock
      Historical data
        North and south poles reversed
        Last reversal less than a million years ago
    Mechanism
      Magma eruption
        Hot magma pushes up
        Hardens into rock
      Magnetic domain alignment
        New rock shows opposite alignment
        Documenting changes in rock
    Scientific Inquiry
      Uncertainty of causes
      Evidence spans hundreds of millions of years
