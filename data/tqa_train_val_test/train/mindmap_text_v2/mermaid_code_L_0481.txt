  Cellular Respiration
    Definition
      Energy conversion in cells
      Glucose and oxygen to carbon dioxide and water
    Purpose
      Provide energy for cellular functions
    ATP
      Adenosine triphosphate
      Energy storage and transfer
      Used for muscle movement
      Energy release when broken down to ADP
    Molecules Used
      Glucose
      Oxygen
      Other polysaccharides like starch
    Plant Respiration
      Yes, plant cells respire
      Produce glucose during photosynthesis
    Energy Extraction
      36 to 38 ATP molecules from one glucose molecule
