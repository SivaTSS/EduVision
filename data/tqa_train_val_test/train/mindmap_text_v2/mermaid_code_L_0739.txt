mindmap
  Temperature and Heat
    Temperature
      Definition
        Average kinetic energy of particles
      Measurement
        Thermometer
          Celsius scale
          Freezing and boiling points
    Thermal Energy
      Definition
        Kinetic energy of moving particles
      Relationship with Temperature
        Cold objects can have more thermal energy
    Heat
      Definition
        Transfer of thermal energy
      Transfer Methods
        Conduction
        Convection
        Radiation
      Relationship with Temperature
        Moves from hot to cold
    Specific Heat
      Definition
        Energy needed to raise temperature of 1 gram by 1C
      Examples
        Glass: 0.84 J/gC
        Copper: 0.39 J/kgC
        Water: 4.18 J/gC
        Sand: 0.67 J/gC
    Real-World Examples
      Beach
        Sand vs. Water temperature
      Golden Gate Bridge
        Expansion and contraction due to temperature changes
