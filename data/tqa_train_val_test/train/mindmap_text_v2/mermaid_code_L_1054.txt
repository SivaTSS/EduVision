mindmap
  Transverse Wave
    Definition
      Particles vibrate perpendicular to wave direction
    Characteristics
      High points are crests
      Low points are troughs
    Examples
      Rope wave
      Guitar string
    S Waves
      Occur during earthquakes
      Travel through underground rocks
      Can travel for hundreds of miles
    Visual Representations
      Diagram of crests and troughs
      Graph of rock motion as sine wave
