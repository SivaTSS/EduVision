mindmap
  Hearing Loss
    What is Hearing
      Ability to sense sound energy
      Organ involved: Ear
    Definition of Deafness
      Total hearing loss
    Causes of Hearing Loss
      Exposure to loud sounds
        Damage to hair cells
        Relationship between sound intensity and hearing loss
          Measured in decibels (dB)
          Example: 100 dB for 15 minutes
    Prevention of Hearing Loss
      Protecting ears from loud sounds
        Use of hearing protectors
          Earplugs
            Muffle sounds
            Suitable for lower noise levels
          Electronic ear protectors
            Identify high-amplitude sounds
            Cause destructive interference
            Recommended for higher noise levels
    Importance of Hearing Protection
      Necessary for workers in loud environments
      Relevant for recreational activities
