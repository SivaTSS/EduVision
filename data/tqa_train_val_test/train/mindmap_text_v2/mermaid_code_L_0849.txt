  Covalent Bonding
    Definition
      Covalent bond is the force of attraction
      Atoms share a pair of valence electrons
    Characteristics
      Forms between nonmetals
      Can be same or different elements
      Results in covalent compounds
    Examples
      Water as a covalent compound
      Oxygen molecule as diatomic
    Valence Electrons
      Nitrogen has five valence electrons
      Oxygen shares two pairs with hydrogen
    Bonding Strength
      Covalent bonds stronger than ionic bonds
    Conductivity
      Covalent compounds do not conduct electricity
    Diatomic Molecules
      Hydrogen, nitrogen, oxygen, halogens
