mindmap
  Peripheral Nervous System
    Parts
      Sensory Division
        Role
          Carries messages from sense organs and internal organs to CNS
        Sensory Neurons
          Receive stimuli from environment
          Send messages to the brain
      Motor Division
        Role
          Carries messages from CNS to internal organs and muscles
        Somatic Nervous System
          Controls voluntary movements
          Example: Waving hand, playing violin
        Autonomic Nervous System
          Controls involuntary functions
          Sympathetic Division
            Prepares body for fight or flight
            Increases heart rate, blood flow
          Parasympathetic Division
            Prepares body for rest and digest
            Controls digestion, heartbeat, breathing
    Functions
      Connects CNS to the rest of the body
      Includes nerves outside the brain and spinal cord
