mindmap
  Wheel and Axle
    Description
      Simple machine
      Two connected rings or cylinders
      Turn around a single center point
    Components
      Inner ring: Axle
      Outer ring: Wheel
    Force Application
      Input force
        Applied to wheel
        Applied to axle
      Output force
        Greater than input force
        Applied over greater distance
    Examples
      Ferris wheel
        Force applied to axle
      Doorknob
        Force applied to wheel
    Mechanical Advantage
      Definition
        Ratio of output force to input force
      Greater than 1
        Input force applied to wheel
      Less than 1
        Input force applied to axle
      Impact on speed
        Wheel turns faster than axle
