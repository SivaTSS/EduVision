  Newtons First Law of Motion
    Definition
      Object at rest remains at rest
      Object in motion remains in motion
      Acted on by unbalanced force
    Application to Skateboarding
      Starting a skateboard
        Push off with foot
        Overcoming friction
      Stopping a skateboard
        Friction as unbalanced force
      Changing direction
        Pressing down on one side
    Historical Context
      Developed by Isaac Newton
      Around 1700
      Known as law of inertia
    Key Concepts
      Inertia
        Tendency to resist change in motion
      Unbalanced forces
        Cause changes in motion
