mindmap
  Radioactivity
    Definition
      Ability to emit charged particles and energy
      General term: radiation
    Discovery
      Antoine Henri Becquerel
        Year: 1896
        Experiment with uranium
        Photographic plate results
      Marie Curie
        Discovered polonium and radium
        Two Nobel Prizes
    Radioactive Elements
      Uranium
      Radium
      Polonium
    Unstable Nuclei
      Too much energy
      Too many protons
      Unstable proton-neutron ratio
    Transformation
      Change in number of protons
      Example: lead (82 protons), gold (79 protons)
