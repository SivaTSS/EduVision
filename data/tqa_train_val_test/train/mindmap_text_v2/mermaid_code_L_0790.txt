mindmap
  acceleration
    definition
      measure of change in velocity
      rate of change
      vector quantity
    examples
      carousel
        changing direction
        changing speed
      skydiver
        free fall
        increasing speed
        parachute effect
    types
      positive acceleration
        increase in speed
      negative acceleration
        decrease in speed (deceleration)
      constant speed with changing direction
        example: driving around a bend
    effects
      feeling acceleration
        pressed against seat when speeding up
        pushed forward when slowing down
        pushed to the left when turning right
        pushed to the right when turning left
