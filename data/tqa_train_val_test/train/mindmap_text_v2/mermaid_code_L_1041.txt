  Synthesis Reactions
    Definition
      Description of synthesis reaction
      General equation: A + B → AB
    Examples
      Sodium and Chlorine
        Reaction: 2Na + Cl2 → 2NaCl
        Product: Sodium Chloride (Table Salt)
      Nitrogen and Hydrogen
        Balanced equation: N2 + 3H2 → 2NH3
      Nitric Oxide and Oxygen
        Balanced equation: 2NO + O2 → 2NO2
    Characteristics
      Easy identification of synthesis reactions
      Formation of a single product from multiple reactants
    Questions
