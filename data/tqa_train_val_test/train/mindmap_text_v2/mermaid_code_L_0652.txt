mindmap
  Puberty and Adolescence
    Puberty
      Definition
        Stage of life when a child becomes sexually mature
      Duration
        Boys: 12 to 18 years
        Girls: 10 to 16 years
      Changes in Boys
        Penis and testes grow
        Testes start making sperm
        Pubic and facial hair grow
        Shoulders broaden
        Voice deepens
      Changes in Girls
        Uterus and ovaries grow
        Ovaries start releasing eggs
        Menstrual cycle begins
        Pubic hair grows
        Hips widen and breasts develop
      Growth Patterns
        Boys grow faster than girls
        Boys: average 10 cm taller by end of puberty
    Adolescence
      Definition
        Period between start of puberty and adulthood
      Changes
        Physical changes from puberty
        Mental changes
          New thinking abilities
          Better logical thinking
          Improved problem-solving
        Emotional changes
          Emotional ups and downs
          Influence of hormone levels
        Social changes
          Increased time spent with peers
          Desire for independence from parents
