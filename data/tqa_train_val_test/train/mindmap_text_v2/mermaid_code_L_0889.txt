mindmap
  Elements
    What is an element
      Definition: Pure substance that cannot be separated
      Naturally occurring elements: 92 known
    Properties of elements
      Unique properties: Different for each element
      Examples: Iron (solid, magnetic), Neon (gas, red glow)
    Historical context
      Aristotle's theory: Four elements (earth, air, water, fire)
      Shift to modern understanding: Discovery of many elements
    Atoms and elements
      Definition of atom: Smallest particle with properties of element
      Similarity of atoms: Atoms of an element are alike
      Example: Carbon atoms (6 protons, 6 electrons)
    Common elements
      Most common in universe: Hydrogen
      Most common in Earth's atmosphere: Nitrogen
      Most common in Earth's crust: Oxygen
      Most common in living things: Carbon
