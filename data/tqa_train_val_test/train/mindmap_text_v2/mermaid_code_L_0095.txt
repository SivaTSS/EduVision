mindmap
  Asteroids
    Definition
      Small rocky bodies
      Orbit the Sun
      Irregularly shaped
    Location
      Asteroid belt
        Between Mars and Jupiter
        Total mass is 4% of Earth's Moon
      Near-Earth asteroids
        Over 4,500 cross Earth's orbit
        500 to 1,000 over 1 km in diameter
    Importance
      Potential impact on Earth
      Linked to mass extinctions
      Early solar system representatives
    Scientific Exploration
      Missions to asteroids
        NASA's DAWN mission
        NEAR Shoemaker probe
      Goals of exploration
        Learn about solar system formation
        Potential mining for minerals
    Risks
      Asteroid impacts
        Average of 5-10 m diameter rocks hit Earth yearly
        Monitoring for potential collisions
