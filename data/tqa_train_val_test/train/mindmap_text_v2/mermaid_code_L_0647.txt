mindmap
  Producers
    Function without sunlight
      Yes: Some can use other energy sources
      No: Most depend on sunlight
    Examples of producers
      Plants
      Algae
      Diatoms
    Autotrophs
      Definition: Organisms that make their own food
    Photosynthesis
      Process: Converts sunlight, CO2, and H2O into glucose
      Ingredients: Sunlight, carbon dioxide, water
      Energy produced: Glucose and oxygen
    Importance to ecosystem
      Food source for consumers
      Energy flow: From producers to consumers
      Essential for ecosystem survival
    Chemosynthesis
      Definition: Process used by some bacteria to produce food
      Energy source: Non-sunlight sources
      Ecosystems: Common in dark environments
