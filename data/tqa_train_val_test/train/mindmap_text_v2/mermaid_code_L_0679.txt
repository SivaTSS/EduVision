mindmap
  Skin
    Structure
      Epidermis
        Outermost layer
        25 to 30 layers of cells
        Contains melanin-producing cells
        No blood vessels
      Dermis
        Under epidermis
        Tough connective tissue
        Contains hair follicles
        Contains sweat glands
        Contains oil glands
        Contains blood vessels
        Contains nerve endings
      Hypodermis
        Below dermis
        Not part of skin
    Functions
      Barrier
        Keeps harmful organisms out
        Prevents water loss
      Temperature control
        Produces sweat
        Evaporative cooling
      Sensory perception
        Senses heat, pressure, cold, pain
      Waste removal
        Removes waste in sweat
      Sun protection
        Melanin blocks sunlight
    Skin as an organ
      Largest organ of the body
      Part of integumentary system
      Composed of skin, hair, nails
    Skin maintenance
      Shedding of dead skin cells
      Replacement of damaged cells
