  Skeletal System Joints
    Joints Definition
      Point where two or more bones meet
      Types of joints
        Fixed Joints
          No movement
          Example: Skull
        Partly Movable Joints
          Limited movement
          Example: Backbone
        Movable Joints
          Most common type
          Examples: Fingers, Toes, Hips, Elbows, Knees
    Types of Movable Joints
      Ball-and-Socket Joint
        Example: Hip, Shoulder
      Hinge Joint
        Example: Knee, Elbow
      Pivot Joint
        Example: Radius and Ulna
      Gliding Joint
        Example: Wrist, Ankles
    Synovial Fluid
      Function: Reduces friction
      Location: Covers surfaces of movable joints
    Knee Cap Function
      Protects knee joint
      Provides stability
