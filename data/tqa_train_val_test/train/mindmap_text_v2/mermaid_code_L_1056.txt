mindmap
  Ultrasound
    Definition
      Sound with frequency higher than human ear can detect
      Frequencies above 20,000 Hz
    Echolocation
      Used by animals like bats and dolphins
      Identifies locations of unseen objects
      Helps find prey and avoid obstacles in the dark
    Sonar
      Stands for Sound Navigation and Ranging
      Locates underwater objects
      Functions as sender and receiver of ultrasound waves
      Distance calculation using speed and time
    Ultrasonography
      Uses reflected ultrasound waves to see inside the body
      Harmless to patients
      Used for examining internal organs and unborn babies
