mindmap
  Screw
    Definition
      Simple machine
      Inclined plane wrapped around cylinder
    Uses
      Move objects to greater depth or height
      Hold things together
        Example: wood pieces
        Example: bottle cap
    Mechanical Advantage
      Always greater than 1
      Output force greater than input force
      Ratio of output to input force
    Examples
      Wood screw
      Bottle cap
      Other examples not mentioned
    Thread Characteristics
      Closer threads
        Greater mechanical advantage
        Easier to turn
      Widely spaced threads
        Harder to turn
        More force needed
