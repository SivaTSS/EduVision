mindmap
  Hydrocarbons
    Definition
      Compounds of carbon and hydrogen
      Simplest carbon-based compounds
    Saturated Hydrocarbons
      Single bonds between carbon atoms
      Also called alkanes
      Types
        Straight-chain
        Branched-chain
        Cyclic
      Isomers
    Unsaturated Hydrocarbons
      At least one double or triple bond
      Types
        Alkenes
          Ends in -ene
          Example: Ethene
        Alkynes
          Ends in -yne
          Example: Ethyne
        Aromatic Hydrocarbons
          Six carbon atoms in a ring
          Example: Benzene
    Properties
      Boiling and melting points
      Influence of size
      Nonpolar nature
    Uses
      Fuels
        Gasoline
        Natural gas
        Diesel fuel
      Manufacturing
        Plastics
        Synthetic fabrics
