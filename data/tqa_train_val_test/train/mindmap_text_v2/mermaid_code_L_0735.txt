mindmap
  Compound Machines
    Definition
      Consists of two or more simple machines
    Examples
      Bicycles
      Cars
      Scissors
      Fishing rods with reels
    Efficiency
      Generally lower than simple machines
      More moving parts lead to more friction
      Lubricants reduce friction
    Mechanical Advantage
      Greater than simple machines
      Equals product of mechanical advantages of components
    Simple Machines in Compound Machines
      Wheels and axles
      Pulleys
      Levers
      Wedges
    Specific Examples
      Scissors
        Two levers
        Two wedges
      Fishing Rod with Reel
        Third-class lever
        Wheel and axle as pulley
