mindmap
  Molecular Compounds
    Covalent Compounds
      Definition
        Nonmetallic elements
        Covalent bonds
        Shared valence electrons
      Properties
        Low boiling points
        Cannot conduct electricity
        May not dissolve in water
    Naming Covalent Compounds
      Rules
        Left-most element first
        Second element with -ide suffix
        Use of prefixes
      Examples
        Hydrogen chloride (HCl)
        Dinitrogen trioxide (N2O3)
        Carbon dioxide (CO2)
    Comparison with Ionic Compounds
      Differences
        Bonding method
        Properties
          Burning ability
          Solubility in water
          Electrical conductivity
