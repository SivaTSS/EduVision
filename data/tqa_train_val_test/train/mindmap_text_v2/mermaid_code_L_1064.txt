  Vision Problems and Corrective Lenses
    Vision Problems
      Myopia
        Definition: Nearsightedness
        Characteristics: Nearby objects clear, distant objects blurry
        Cause: Eyeball longer than normal
        Correction: Concave lenses
        Additional Info: Squinting may improve focus
      Hyperopia
        Definition: Farsightedness
        Characteristics: Distant objects clear, nearby objects blurry
        Cause: Eyeball shorter than normal
        Correction: Convex lenses
        Additional Info: More likely to need glasses for reading
    Corrective Lenses
      Types
        Contact Lenses
        Eyeglasses
        Laser Surgery
      Function
        Reshape outer layer of the eye
