  Distance
    Definition
      Length of route between two points
    SI Unit
      Meter (m)
      Examples
        Short distances: centimeters (cm)
        Long distances: kilometers (km)
    Measurement
      Using maps
        Scale to find distances
        Example: Distance from Jordan's house to school is 2 kilometers
    Race Distance
      Length of track between starting and finishing lines
      Example: 100-meter sprint
