  Scientific Law
    Definition
      Statement describing what always happens under certain conditions
    Examples
      Newton's Three Laws of Motion
        First Law
        Second Law
        Third Law
      Law of Conservation of Mass
      Law of Conservation of Energy
      Law of Conservation of Momentum
    Differences from Theories
      Laws state what always happens
      Theories answer why questions
    Relationship with Theories
      Laws provide basis for theories
      Theories explain why laws exist
    Examples of Laws in Action
      Bumper Cars
      Hitting a Ball with a Bat
      Pushing a Swing
    Predictive Value
      Allows prediction of future events
    Limitations
      Do not explain why things happen
