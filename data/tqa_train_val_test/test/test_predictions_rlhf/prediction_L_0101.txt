mindmap
  Blizzards
    Definition
      Temperature
        Below -7 C (20 F)
        -12 C (10 F) for severe blizzard
      Wind
        Greater than 56 kmh (35 mph)
        -72 kmh (45 mph) for severe blizzard
      Visibility
        Snow so heavy that visibility is 2/5 km (1/4 mile) or less for at least three hours
        Near zero visibility for severe blizzard
    Formation
      Mid-latitude cyclone
        Jet stream brings cold air into contact with warm moist air
      Lake-effect snow
        Cold polar air absorbs moisture over Great Lakes
        Dumps snow downwind
    Occurrence
      Middle latitudes and toward poles
      Winter season
      U.S. locations
        Great Lakes region
        Northeast
    Survival
      Stay indoors
      Avoid travel
      Dress warmly
      Have emergency supplies
      Listen to weather reports
