mindmap
  Scientific Theories
    Definition
      Explanation of natural world
      Based on repeated observations
    Evidence
      Direct, physical observation
      Measurable or quantifiable
      Data from experiments
    Hypothesis
      Proposed explanation
      Tries to explain observation
    Scientific Law
      Statement describing natural phenomena
      Always happens under certain conditions
    Scientific Theory
      Well-established explanation
      Tested and confirmed by many
      Produces understanding of world
    Germ Theory of Disease
      Explanation of disease spread
      Confirmed by evidence
    Cell Theory
      Explanation of cell structure
      Confirmed by evidence
    Theory of Evolution
      Explanation of species development
      Confirmed by evidence
    Importance
      Helps understand natural world
      Can change with new evidence
