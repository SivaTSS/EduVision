mindmap
  Basic and Applied Science
    Basic Science
      Understanding fundamental problems
      Examples
        - Understanding how cells work
        - Investigating how glucose is turned into cellular energy
    Applied Science
      Solving practical problems
      Examples
        - Medicine
        - Designing windmills to capture wind energy
    Relationship
      Applied Science uses basic science
      Basic Science informs Applied Science
    Emphasis on Applied Science
      Need for more emphasis
      Improving human health and technology
    Basic Research vs. Applied Research
      Basic Research
        - Increasing human knowledge of nature
        - Examples
          - Understanding how the body makes cholesterol
          - Determining how elevated blood glucose levels can be harmful
      Applied Research
        - Applying basic scientific knowledge to solve practical problems
        - Examples
          - Medicine
          - Designing windmills to capture wind energy
