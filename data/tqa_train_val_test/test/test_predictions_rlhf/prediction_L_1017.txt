mindmap
  Scientific Process
    Role of Investigation
      Produces evidence
      Answers questions
      Advances science
    Steps of Scientific Method
      Ask a question
      Do background research
      Construct a hypothesis
      Test the hypothesis
        Do an experiment
      Analyze the data
        Draw a conclusion
      Report the results
    Example: Cody's Investigation
      Observation: Cereal contains iron
      Question: Is there enough iron for magnet attraction?
      Hypothesis: Iron content too low
      Test: Use strong magnet on cereal flake
      Conclusion: Hypothesis incorrect
      Next step: Report results to other scientists
