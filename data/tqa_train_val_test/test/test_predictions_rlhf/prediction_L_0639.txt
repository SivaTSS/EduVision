mindmap
  Predation
    Definition
      Feeding on another living organism
    Types
      True Predation
        Killing and eating prey
        Examples: Lions, Bottlenose Dolphins, Snakes
      Grazing
        Eating part of prey without killing
        Examples: Cows, Fish, Kelp
    Adaptations
      Predators
        Camouflage
        Speed
        Heightened Senses
      Prey
        Camouflage
        Mimicry
    Keystone Species
      Definition
        Species with large effect on ecosystem balance
      Example
        Wolves and Deer/Rabbits
    Prey Adaptations for Avoidance
      Camouflage
      Mimicry
    Symbiosis
      Parasitism (not covered in text)
    Consequences
      Balance of Organisms
      Dominance of Species
      Effect on Ecosystem
