mindmap
  Weather versus Climate
    Weather
      Definition
        Change from day to day
        Directly related to energy
      Factors
        Air temperature
        Air pressure
        Fog
        Humidity
        Cloud cover
        Precipitation
        Wind speed and direction
    Climate
      Definition
        Long-term average of weather
        Predictable
      Factors
        Angle of the Sun
        Cloud cover
        Air pressure
        Energy
        Position relative to factors
    Relationship between Weather and Climate
      Weather can change rapidly
      Climate changes slowly
      Climate determines weather patterns
    Importance of Weather and Climate
      Weather affects daily life
      Climate affects long-term planning
      Climate change affects the planet
