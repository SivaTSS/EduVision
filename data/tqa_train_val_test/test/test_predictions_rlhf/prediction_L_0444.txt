mindmap
  Amphibians
    Definition
      Group of vertebrates
      Live in both water and on land
    Characteristics
      Ectothermic
        External temperature affects body temperature
      Metamorphosis
        Life cycle stage changes
      Larval stage
        Aquatic, gilled stage
      Adult stage
        Terrestrial, lung-breathing stage
    Adaptations
      Skin
        Prevents water loss
      Eyelids
        Adaptation to land
      Eardrum
        Separates external and middle ear
      Tail
        Disappears in adulthood
    Habitats
      Freshwater environments
        Streams, rivers, lakes, swamps, ponds
      Drier environments
        Forests, meadows, marshes
    Reproduction
      Sexual reproduction
        External, internal, or internal fertilization
    Orders
      Urodela
        Salamanders, newts
      Anura
        Frogs, toads
      Apoda
        Caecilians
