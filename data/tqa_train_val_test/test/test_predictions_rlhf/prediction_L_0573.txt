mindmap
  Innate Behavior of Animals
    Definition
      Innate behavior or instinct
      Behavior that occurs naturally in all animals of a given species
    Examples
      Honeybees doing the waggle dance
      Spiders spinning a web
      Cats hunting
      Birds building nests
    Reflex Behaviors in Humans
      Sucking reflex
      Grasp reflex
    Innate Behavior in Animals
      Honeybees dancing
      Gulls pecking at red spots
      Gaping in birds
      Egg rolling in water birds
    Benefits of Innate Behavior
      Increases chances of survival
      Predictable behavior
      Important actions like eating and caring for young
    Conclusion
      All animals have innate behaviors
      Innate behaviors help animals survive
