mindmap
  Primate Order
    Definition
      Mammal class
      Placental mammals
      Includes lemurs, lorises, tarsiers, monkeys, apes, humans
    Traits
      Five digits on each extremity
      Stereoscopic vision
      Arboreal origins
    Habitats
      Tropical rainforests
      Central and South America, Africa, South Asia
    Diet
      Omnivorous
      Prefer fruit but consume variety of plant and animal foods
    Prosimian vs Non-Prosimian Primates
      Prosimians: smaller, fewer, include lemurs, lorises
      Non-Prosimians: tarsiers, New World monkeys, Old World monkeys, apes, humans
    Development
      Slower rates of development
      Longer maturation period
      More time to learn from elders
    Learning Ability
      High level of intelligence
      Ability to learn new behaviors
    Examples
      Mouse lemur
      Gorilla
      Gibbon
      Chimpanzee
