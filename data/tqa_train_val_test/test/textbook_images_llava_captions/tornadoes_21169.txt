This is a map of the United States showing the number of tornadoes that have occurred in each state from 1950 to 1999. The map is color-coded to indicate the number of tornadoes that have occurred in each state. The states with the highest number of tornadoes are shown in red, while the states with the lowest number of tornadoes are shown in yellow. The map is based on data from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) and the National Severe Storms Laboratory (NSSL).
