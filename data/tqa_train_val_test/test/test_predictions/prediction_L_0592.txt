mindmap
  Mammal Characteristics
    Definition
      Warm-blooded vertebrates
      Range from bats to whales
    Unique Traits
      Hair or fur
      Sweat glands
      Mammary glands
      Three middle ear bones
      Neocortex region in brain
      Specialized teeth
      Four-chambered heart
    Reproduction
      Viviparous (live young)
        Monotremes (egg-laying)
          Platypus, echidnas
      Marsupials (pouch-bearing)
        Kangaroos, koalas, possums
      Placental mammals (uterus)
        Humans, rabbits, squirrels
    Adaptations
      Land to ocean (whales, dolphins)
      Diverse habitats
        Ocean, sky, prairies, savanna
    Mammal Classification
      Approx. 5,400 species
      29 orders, 153 families, 1,200 genera
    Mammal Types
      Viviparous
        Monotremes
        Marsupials
        Placental mammals
    Mammal Examples
      Bumblebee bat, blue whale
      Dogs, cats, eleph
