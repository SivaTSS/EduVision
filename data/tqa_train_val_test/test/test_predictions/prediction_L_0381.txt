mindmap
  Animal Behavior
    Definition
      Interaction with environment or other animals
      Behaviors can be alone or with others
    Purpose
      Increase fitness
        Survival
        Reproduction
    Evolution
      Genes control behaviors that increase fitness
      Natural Selection
    Innate Behavior
      Occurs naturally in all animals of a species
      No practice needed
      Predictable
        Same behavior in all members of species
    Examples
      Honeybees waggle dance
      Human babies grasp reflex
    Learned Behavior
      Behavior occurs after experience or practice
      Flexible
        Adapt to changing conditions
    Learning Methods
      Habituation
        Get used to something after repeated exposure
      Observational Learning
        Learn by watching and copying someone else
      Conditioning
        Reward or punishment
      Play
        Fun but may learn important behaviors
      Insight Learning
        Learn from past experiences and reasoning
        Sudden flash of insight
    Communication
      Sharing information with other animals
      Purposes
        Reproduction
        Survival
