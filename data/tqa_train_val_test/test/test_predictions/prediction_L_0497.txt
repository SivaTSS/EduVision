  Crustaceans
    Definition
      Arthropod subphylum
      Includes crabs, lobsters, shrimp, krill, woodlice
    Characteristics
      Exoskeleton
        May be bound together
        Carapace: thick back shield
      Main body cavity
        Expanded circulatory system
        Heart near back
      Digestive system
        Straight tube with gastric mill
        Digestive glands
      Waste removal
        Structures like kidneys
      Reproduction
        Separate sexes
        Sexual reproduction using eggs and sperm
      Shedding outer skeleton
        Vulnerable during growth period
    Classification
      Six classes
        Branchiopoda
          Small, freshwater animals
          Feed on plankton and detritus
        Remipedia
          Blind organisms in deep caves
        Cephalocarida
          Small crustaceans
        Maxillopoda
          Ostracoda
            Small animals with bivalve shells
          Malacostraca
            Largest class
            Most familiar animals
            Diverse body forms
