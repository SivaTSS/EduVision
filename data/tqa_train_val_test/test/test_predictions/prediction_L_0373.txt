mindmap
  Echinoderms and Invertebrate Chordates
    Echinoderms
      Definition
        Spiny skin
        Endoskeleton
      Characteristics
        Radial symmetry
        Tube feet with suckers
        Network of internal canals
      Reproduction
        Asexual: fission
        Sexual: separate sexes, fertilization outside body
    Chordates
      Definition
        Notochord
        Post-anal tail
        Hollow dorsal nerve cord
        Pharyngeal slits
      Characteristics
        Three embryonic cell layers
        Segmented body
        Complete coelom
        Bilateral symmetry
        Complete digestive system
        Central nervous system
        Circulatory system
    Invertebrate Chordates
      Tunicates
        Definition
          Ocean-dwelling invertebrates
          About 3000 species
        Characteristics
          Live in shallow water
          Larvae can swim
          Adults are filter feeders
          Reproduction: sexual and asexual
      Lancelets
        Definition
          Invertebrate chordates
          About 25 species
        Characteristics
          Live in shallow ocean water
          Larvae can swim
          Adults are filter feeders
