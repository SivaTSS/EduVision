  Reptiles
    Definition
      Ectothermic vertebrates
      Four-legged animals
      Produce amniotic eggs
    Terrestrial Adaptations
      Scales for protection
      Efficient lungs
      Centralized nervous system
    Reproduction
      Sexual reproduction
      Internal fertilization
      Oviparous (eggs hatch outside body)
    Classification
      Four orders: Crocodilia, Sphenodontia, Squamata, Testudines
    Habitats
      Aquatic and terrestrial
    Diet
      Carnivores, herbivores, omnivores
    Ecosystem Roles
      Top predators in ecosystems
      Prey on various animals
