mindmap
  Animal-like Protists (Protozoa)
    Definition
      Single-celled eukaryotes
      Share traits with animals
    Characteristics
      Heterotrophs: eat outside food
      Move by flagella, cilia, or pseudopodia
      Size: 0.01-0.5mm
    Classification
      Four groups: Flagellates, Ciliates, Sporozoans
    Flagellates
      Long flagella (tails)
      Rotate propeller-like
      Example: Trypanosoma (African sleeping sickness)
    Ciliates
      Move by cilia (thin tail-like projections)
      Example: Paramecium
    Sporozoans
      Produce spores
      Example: Toxoplasma
    Examples
      Amoeba (pseudopodia)
      Paramecium (ciliates)
      Trypanosoma (flagellates)
      Toxoplasma (sporozoans)
