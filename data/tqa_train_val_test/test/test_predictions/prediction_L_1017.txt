mindmap
  Scientific Process
    Role of Investigation
      Produces evidence
      Answers questions
      Advances science
    Steps of Scientific Method
      Ask a question
      Do background research
      Construct a hypothesis
      Test the hypothesis
        Do an experiment
      Analyze data
        Draw a conclusion
      Report results
    Example: Cody's Investigation
      Observation: Cereal contains iron
      Question: Is there enough iron for magnet attraction?
      Hypothesis: Iron content is too low
      Evidence: Cereal attracted by strong magnet
      Conclusion: Hypothesis is incorrect
    Scientific Investigations
      Field-specific methods
      Guided by theories and questions
    Importance of Investigations
      Answers questions
      Leads to new questions
      Advances science
