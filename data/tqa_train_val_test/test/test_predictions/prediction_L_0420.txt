mindmap
  Biodiversity
    Definition
      Variety of life and its processes
      Includes genetic differences among species
      Range of communities and ecosystems
    Importance to People
      Economic benefits
        Food
        Products (dyes, rubber, fibers, paper, adhesives, timber)
        Medical drugs
        Inspiration for technology
      Ecosystem services
        Maintain Earth's atmosphere
        Protect soil
        Decompose organic matter
        Control insect pests
        Pollinate flowering plants
    Importance to Ecosystems
      Increases productivity and stability
      Ensures survival during environmental change
      Provides other ecosystem services
    Extinction
      Definition
        Complete dying out of a species
      Mass Extinctions
        Five mass extinctions in Earth's history
        Caused by major geologic and climatic events
        Wiped out the dinosaurs 65 million years ago
      Sixth Mass Extinction
        Caused mainly by human actions
        Species going extinct at the fastest rate since the dinosaurs died out
        Dozens of species going extinct every day
        If this rate continues, as many as half of all remaining species could go extinct by 2050
    Causes of the Sixth Mass Extinction
      Habitat Loss
