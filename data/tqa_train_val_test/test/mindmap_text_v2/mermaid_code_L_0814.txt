mindmap
  Boyle's Law
    Definition
      States that decreasing gas volume increases pressure
    Explanation
      Pressure increases due to more collisions of gas molecules
    Example
      Squeezing a balloon
        Prediction: Balloon pressure increases as volume decreases
    Related Concepts
      Gas Laws
        Charles' Law
        Amontons' Law
    Historical Context
      Named after Robert Boyle
      Boyle's experiments in the 1660s
      Father of modern chemistry
    Visualization
      Inverse relationship between volume and pressure
        Graph representation
          X-axis: Volume (V)
          Y-axis: Pressure (P)
