mindmap
  Weather vs Climate
    Weather
      Definition
        Atmosphere conditions at a specific time and place
      Characteristics
        Air temperature
        Air pressure
        Humidity
        Precipitation
        Wind speed and direction
        Fog
        Cloud cover
      Changeability
        Rapid changes
        Daily variations
      Energy Source
        The Sun
    Climate
      Definition
        Long-term average of weather
      Characteristics
        Air temperature
        Humidity
        Wind speed and direction
        Precipitation type, quantity, frequency
      Changeability
        Slow changes
        Predictable patterns
      Determining Factors
        Angle of the Sun
        Cloud cover likelihood
        Air pressure
        Energy distribution over time
    Comparison
      Weather vs Climate
        Weather changes quickly
        Climate changes slowly
        Weather is less predictable
        Climate is more stable
