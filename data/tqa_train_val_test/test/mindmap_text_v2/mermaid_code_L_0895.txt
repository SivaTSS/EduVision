mindmap
  Ethics in Science
    What is Ethics?
      Definition: Deciding what's right and wrong
    Ethical Rules in Science
      Honesty in Reporting
        Avoid fabricating or altering results
      Objectivity
        Avoid bias in research
      Carefulness
        Minimize errors in data
      Informed Consent
        Inform community about risks
      Humane Treatment
        Care for living subjects
    Ethical Dilemmas
      Example: Pacific Yew
        Right: Save lives with cancer drug
        Wrong: Risk extinction of the tree
        Solution: Synthesize drug in labs
    Science and Personal Decisions
      Influence of Scientific Evidence
        Example: Climate change and driving
      Ethical Solutions
        Carpooling to reduce impact
        Adjusting thermostat to save energy
