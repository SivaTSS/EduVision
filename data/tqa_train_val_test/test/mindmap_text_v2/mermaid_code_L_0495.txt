mindmap
  Consumers and Decomposers
    Decomposers
      Role in Ecosystem
        Break down dead organisms
        Release nutrients
        Recycle nutrients
      Examples
        Bacteria
        Fungi
        Mushrooms
      Importance
        Stability of ecosystem
        Nutrient cycling
        Without them, waste accumulates
    Consumers
      Definition
        Obtain energy from other organisms
      Types
        Herbivores
          Eat producers
          Examples: rabbits, deer, caterpillars
        Carnivores
          Eat other animals
          Examples: snakes, hawks
        Omnivores
          Eat both producers and consumers
          Examples: humans, dogs, bears, raccoons
      Importance
        Essential for energy flow in ecosystems
