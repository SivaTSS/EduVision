mindmap
  Life Science
    Definition
      Study of life and living organisms
      Also called biology
    Scope
      Complex and diverse
      Divided into fields
    Fields of Life Science
      Ecology
        Focus: interactions of organisms with each other and their environment
      Botany
        Focus: plants
      Zoology
        Focus: animals
      Microbiology
        Focus: microorganisms
      Entomology
        Focus: insects
      Cell Biology
        Focus: cells of living things
      Physiology
        Focus: tissues and organs
      Genetics
        Focus: genes, traits, and inheritance
      Epidemiology
        Focus: causes of diseases and how they spread
      Paleontology
        Focus: fossils and evolution
    Basic Theories
      Cell Theory
        All organisms made of cells
        Cells come from pre-existing cells
      Theory of Evolution by Natural Selection
        Explains diversity of living things
        Populations change over time
    Research Types
      Basic Science
        Aim: discover new knowledge
        Example: studying yeast cells
      Applied Science
        Aim: find solutions to practical problems
        Example: cancer treatment drugs
