mindmap
  Climate and its Causes
    Definition
      Average weather over many years
      Includes temperature and precipitation
    Latitude
      Distance from equator
      Temperature changes with latitude
        Highest at equator
        Lowest at poles
    Oceans
      Influence on coastal climate
        Mild temperatures
        High precipitation
      Ocean currents
        Warm and cold water effects
        Impact on atmosphere and climate
    Mountains
      Effect on temperature
        Cooler at higher altitudes
      Influence on precipitation
        Rain shadow effect
    Global Air Currents
      Affect precipitation
      Create global winds
        Prevailing winds impact climate
    Coastal vs Inland Climates
      Coastal
        Mild temperatures
        High precipitation
      Inland
        Greater temperature extremes
        Lower precipitation
