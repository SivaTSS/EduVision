mindmap
  development of theories
    scientific theory
      explanation of complex phenomena
      predicts future events
      supported by evidence
      constantly tested and revised
      simpler model of reality
    scientific law
      describes consistent phenomena
      does not explain why
      may have exceptions
    relationship between theories and laws
      theories explain why
      laws describe what happens
      theories can become laws
      laws cannot become theories
    hypothesis
      initial idea to be tested
      supported or refuted by evidence
      can become a theory
    importance in science
      theories provide reliable explanations
      laws describe consistent outcomes
      no hierarchy of importance
