mindmap
  Archaeans
    Definition
      Prokaryotes in Archaea Domain
      First discovered in extreme environments
      Classified as bacteria until 1970s
    Types of Extremophiles
      Halophiles
        Thrive in salty environments
        Found in Great Salt Lake and Dead Sea
      Hyperthermophiles
        Thrive in high temperatures
        Can reproduce at 122°C (252°F)
      Acidophiles
        Thrive in acidic environments
        Found in acid mine drainage and volcanic vents
      Alkaliphiles
        Thrive in basic environments
        Found in Mono Lake
    Habitats
      Found in diverse environments
      Soils, bodies of water, marshlands
      Common in oceans and human bodies
    Ecological Roles
      Important decomposers
      Help recycle carbon and nitrogen
      Live in close relationships with other organisms
      Methanogens in cows
        Aid in digestion of cellulose
        Produce methane as waste
