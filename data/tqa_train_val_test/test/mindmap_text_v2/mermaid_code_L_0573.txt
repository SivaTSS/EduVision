mindmap
  Innate Behavior of Animals
    Definition
      Innate behavior is natural to all animals of a species
      Also known as instinct
    Examples
      Honeybees
        Waggle dance to indicate food source
      Birds
        Nest building
        Gaping behavior in chicks
        Egg rolling in graylag geese
      Spiders
        Web spinning
      Cats
        Natural-born hunters
    Human Behaviors
      Reflex behaviors
        Sucking reflex in newborns
        Grasp reflex in babies
    Characteristics
      Predictable actions
      No need for learning or practice
      Important for survival
    Comparisons
      Learned vs. Innate
        Moon snail behavior
        Cockle behavior
        Spider crab vs. stingray behaviors
