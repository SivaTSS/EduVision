  Ecology Levels of Organization
    Population
      Definition: Group of organisms of the same species in an area
      Characteristics: Interact with one another
    Community
      Definition: All populations of different species in an area
      Characteristics: Composed of biotic factors
    Ecosystem
      Definition: Living organisms and non-living aspects in an area
      Components: Biotic and abiotic factors
    Biosphere
      Definition: Part of the planet with living organisms
      Includes: Most of Earth, oceans, atmosphere
    Species
      Definition: Group of genetically related individuals that can breed
      Example: Homo sapiens (sapiens is the species name)
    Ecological Questions
      Individual Level: How do zebras keep water in their bodies?
      Population Level: What causes the growth of a zebra population?
      Community Level: How does a disturbance affect mammal species?
      Ecosystem Level: How does fire affect food availability in ecosystems?
