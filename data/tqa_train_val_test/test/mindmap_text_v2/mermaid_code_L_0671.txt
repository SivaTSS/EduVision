    Evidence
      Direct observation

    Scientific Theory
      Explanation of natural world
      Based on repeated observations
      Developed from hypotheses
      Supported by evidence
      Examples: atomic theory, germ theory, cell theory, theory of evolution
    Scientific Law
      Statement of consistent natural phenomena
      Developed from extensive evidence
      Example: law of gravity
    Hypothesis
      Proposed explanation for an observation
      Tested through experiments
