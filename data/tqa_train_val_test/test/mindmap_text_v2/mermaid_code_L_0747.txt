      Includes methods, processes, and devices

      Steps
        Identify problem
        Research solutions
        Develop designs
        Create model
        Test model
        Finalize design
      Constraints
        Laws of nature
        Material properties
        Production costs
        Ethical concerns

      Different goals
        Science: Increase knowledge
        Technology: Practical application of knowledge

        Scientific knowledge aids technology
        Technology aids scientific exploration
    Impact on Society
      Technology influences society
      Society sets direction for technology

        Solar-powered cars
