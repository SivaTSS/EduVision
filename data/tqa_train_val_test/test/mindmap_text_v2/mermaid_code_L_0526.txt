mindmap
  Frog and Toads
    Characteristics
      Frogs
        Long hind legs
        Smooth and moist skin
        Special pads on toes
      Toads
        Shorter legs
        Drier skin with warts
        Heavier body
    Habitat
      Frogs
        Prefer water or moist environments
        Found globally, especially in tropical rainforests
      Toads
        More terrestrial
    Life Cycle
      Eggs
        Laid in water
        Develop into tadpoles
      Tadpoles
        Gills and tail
        No legs
        Similar to fish
      Adult Frogs
        Transition from tadpole to adult
        Mating calls
    Behavior
      Mating
        Males "ribbit" to attract females
        Glue used in mating
        Problems with glue
      Feeding
        Carnivorous diet
        Catching insects with long tongue
        Tongue mechanics
    Adaptations
      Jumping
        Long hind legs and ankle bones
        Short backbone
      Skin
        Moist and permeable
        Breathing through skin
      Eyes
        Three eyelid membranes
        Tympanum for hearing
