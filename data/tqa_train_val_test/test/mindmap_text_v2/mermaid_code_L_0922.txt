mindmap
  Hypothesis
    Role in Science
      Definition: Educated guess
      Testable and falsifiable
    Criteria of Scientific Hypothesis
      Testable
        Observations can agree or disagree
      Falsifiable
        Must be possible to show it is false
    Examples of Hypotheses
      Acids turn red litmus paper blue
      All life in the universe exists on Earth
    Testing Hypotheses
      Example: Dropping objects
        Testable: Yes
        Falsifiable: Yes
      Example: Invisible creatures
        Testable: No
        Falsifiable: No
    Limitations of Proving Hypotheses
      Impossible to examine all cases
      One exception can disprove
      Gathering evidence increases likelihood of truth
