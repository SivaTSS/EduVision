mindmap
  Nature of Science
    Define Science
      Way of learning about the natural world
      Based on evidence and logic
    Goal of Science
      Understand how and why things happen
      Advance knowledge through evidence
    Examples of Science Advancements
      Fireworks
        Invented 2000 years ago
        Explanation required knowledge of elements and chemical reactions
      Newton's Law of Universal Gravitation
        Introduced in 1666
        Explains attraction between objects
        Influenced understanding of planetary motion
      Atomic Structure
        Introduced in early 1800s
        Understanding expanded over two centuries
      Wegener's Theory of Continental Drift
        Proposed in early 1900s
        Initially ridiculed, later accepted
        Supported by fossil and rock evidence
