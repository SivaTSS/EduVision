mindmap
  Symbiosis
    Definition
      Close interaction between different species
      At least one species benefits
    Types
      Mutualism
        Both species benefit
        Examples
          Herbivores and intestinal bacteria
          Clownfish and sea anemone
          Plover bird and African crocodile
          Ostrich and zebra
      Commensalism
        One species benefits, other unaffected
        Examples
          Spiders and trees
          Cattle egrets and livestock
          Tigers and golden jackals
      Parasitism
        Parasitic species benefits, host harmed
        Examples
          Hookworm
            Lives in host's intestine
            Causes pain
            Can kill host (rare)
    Importance
      Necessary for survival of one or both organisms
