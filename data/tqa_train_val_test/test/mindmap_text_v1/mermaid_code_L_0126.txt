mindmap
  Scientific Theories and Laws
    Definition
      Theory: Explanation of complex phenomena
      Law: Description of consistent phenomena
    Comparison
      Hypothesis
        Initial idea to be tested
      Theory
        Supported by evidence
        Predicts future events
      Law
        Always applies under specific conditions
    Relationships
      Can a theory become a law?
      Can a law become a theory?
      Can a hypothesis become a law or theory?
    Importance in Science
      Hypothesis
      Theory
      Law
    Characteristics
      Theory
        Strong evidence
        Constantly tested and revised
        Simpler model of reality
      Law
        Applies under same conditions
        May have exceptions
