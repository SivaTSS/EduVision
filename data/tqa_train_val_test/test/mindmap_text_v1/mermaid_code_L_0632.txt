mindmap
  Plant Classification
    Nonvascular Plants
      Characteristics
        - First to evolve
        - No vascular tissue
      Examples
        - Mosses
        - Liverworts
        - Hornworts
    Seedless Vascular Plants
      Characteristics
        - Vascular tissue present
        - No seeds
      Examples
        - Ferns
        - Whisk ferns
        - Club mosses
        - Horsetails
    Gymnosperms
      Characteristics
        - Seeds present
        - No flowers
      Examples
        - Redwood
        - Fir
        - Cypress
    Angiosperms
      Characteristics
        - Vascular tissue present
        - Seeds present
        - Flowers present
      Examples
        - Magnolia trees
        - Roses
        - Tulips
        - Tomatoes
    Evolutionary Order
      - Nonvascular plants
      - Seedless vascular plants
      - Gymnosperms
      - Angiosperms
