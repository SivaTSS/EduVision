mindmap
  Scientific Theories
    Evidence
      Definition
        Direct observation
        Measurable data
        Experimental results
      Examples
        Apple falling (gravity)
        Bear skeleton (presence of bears)
    Scientific Theory
      Definition
        Explanation of natural world
        Based on repeated observations
      Development
        Hypothesis formation
        Evidence collection through experiments
      Characteristics
        Well-established explanations
        Supported by extensive evidence
        Subject to change with new evidence
      Examples
        Atomic theory
        Germ theory of disease
        Cell theory
        Theory of evolution
    Scientific Law
      Definition
        Statement of consistent natural phenomena
      Development
        Based on extensive collected information
      Characteristics
        Describes what always happens under certain conditions
