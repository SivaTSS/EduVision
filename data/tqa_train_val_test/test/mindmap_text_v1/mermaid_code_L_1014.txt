mindmap
  Scientific Law
    Definition
      - Statement of consistent outcomes under specific conditions
    Examples
      - Newton's Laws of Motion
        - First Law
        - Second Law
        - Third Law
      - Other Laws
        - Law of Universal Gravitation
        - Law of Conservation of Mass
        - Law of Conservation of Energy
        - Law of Conservation of Momentum
    Comparison
      - Scientific Laws vs. Theories
        - Laws describe what happens
        - Theories explain why it happens
      - Contrast with Human-Made Laws
        - Traffic laws vs. scientific laws
    Applications
      - Predictive Nature
        - Example: Hitting a softball
      - Limitations
        - Do not explain underlying reasons
