mindmap
  Protist Characteristics
    Definition
      Kingdom Protista
      Eukaryotic organisms
      Not animals, plants, or fungi
    Common Features
      Eukaryotic cells
      Mostly unicellular
      Some multicellular (e.g., kelp)
      Prefer aquatic or moist environments
    Classification
      Animal-like Protists
        Heterotrophs
        Ability to move
      Plant-like Protists
        Autotrophs
        Photosynthesize
      Fungi-like Protists
        Heterotrophs
        Cell walls
        Reproduce by spores
    Historical Context
      Established by Ernst Haeckel in 1866
      Accepted in the 1960s
      Referred to as "junk drawer" kingdom
    Reproduction
      Varies among protists
