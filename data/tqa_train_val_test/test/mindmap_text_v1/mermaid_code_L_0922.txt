mindmap
  Hypothesis
    Role in Science
      Definition
        Educated guess
        Testable and falsifiable
    Criteria of Scientific Hypothesis
      Testable
        Observations can agree/disagree
        Example: "Invisible creatures"
      Falsifiable
        Can be proven false
        Example: "Other planets with life"
    Examples
      Falling Objects
        Statement: "Two objects dropped at the same time will reach the ground at the same time"
        Testable
        Falsifiable
      Limitations
        Impossible to prove true
        Need to test all combinations
        Evidence supports likelihood of truth
