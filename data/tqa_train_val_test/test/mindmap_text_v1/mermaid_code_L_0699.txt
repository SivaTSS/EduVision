mindmap
  Phototropism
    Definition
      Tropism
        Growth toward or away from a stimulus
    Types
      Phototropism
        Response to light
      Gravitropism
        Response to gravity
      Thigmotropism
        Response to touch
    Mechanism
      Auxins
        Hormones causing directional growth
        Movement to one side of the stem
        Rapid cell growth on one side
    Examples
      Leaves and stems
        Grow toward light
      Roots
        Grow away from light
      Plant on its side
        Stem curves upward
      Tendrils
        Twist around poles
    Experiments
      Signal location
        Growing tip of the plant
      Signal migration
        Up and down plant shoot
      Agar block
        Substance responsible for response
    Questions
      What hormone causes phototropic response?
      How do roots know to grow downward?
