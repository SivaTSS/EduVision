mindmap
  Scientific Theory
    Definition
      Broad explanation
      Supported by evidence
    Importance
      Connects observations
      Explains phenomena
    Comparison
      Science vs. Everyday Language
        Scientific theory: well-supported explanation
        Everyday language: casual use
    Examples
      Dalton's Atomic Theory
        All substances consist of atoms
        Atoms of an element are identical
        Compounds have fixed proportions
      Einstein's Theory of Gravity
      Kinetic Theory of Matter
      Wave-Particle Theory of Light
    Formation
      Law of Parsimony
        Simplest theory preferred
        Fewer assumptions = higher correctness
