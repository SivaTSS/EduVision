mindmap
  Animal-like Protists
    Definition
      - Single-celled eukaryotes
      - Heterotrophs
      - Size: 0.01-0.5 mm
    Taxonomy
      - Five Kingdom System
        - Proposed by: Whittaker
      - Kingdom Protista
    Groups of Protozoa
      - Flagellates
        - Movement: Long flagella
        - Example: Trypanosoma (African sleeping sickness)
      - Ciliates
        - Movement: Cilia
        - Example: Paramecium
      - Sporozoans
        - Movement: None
        - Reproduction: Spores
        - Example: Toxoplasma
      - Amoeboids
        - Movement: Pseudopodia
        - Example: Amoeba
    Characteristics
      - Movement methods
        - Flagella
        - Cilia
        - Pseudopodia
        - Non-motile
      - Disease-causing potential
